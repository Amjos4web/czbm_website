PASTOR J.O ADESINA

General Overseer

I am pleased to welcome you to the online platform of Christos Zoe Bible Ministry. Established in 2004, with a vision to reach the world with the message of complete freedom through Christ, Christos Zoe Bible Ministry, together with its church arm; Christ-centred Life Bible Churches has been growing like the proverbial mustard seed and the Lord has been using her to touch many lives both within and outside this nation with experiences of salvation, sanctification, deliverance and healings trailing the preaching of the Word. Christos Zoe Bible Ministry is a platform the Lord is using to help rediscover the ancient paths that lead to life; and through the multifaceted nature of the work the Lord is doing, victorious and heavenly-minded Christian soldiers are being raised this end-time evangelistic challenges. To further achieve this, the Lord has graciously provided this online platform to know more about Christ, about the ministry and to provide a platform for you to access numerous spiritual resources that will enrich your spiritual life, deepen your walk with God and make you fit for heaven at last. Added to these are also valuable tools and links that will add value to your life, building in you the total man and helping you to a victorious and fulfilled life. All these are powered by the Holy Spirit through His Word. Among other resources available for you on this platform includes: Life transforming Videos & Audio Messages, live from our you-tube channel, Recorded Messages, Image Galleries, Sunday School Manuals, Fresh Fire devotionals, Books and Manuscripts, Question and Answer Disqus forum, Newsletters & Spiritual blogs. These valuable resources will certainly help you to grow to become more like Him.
